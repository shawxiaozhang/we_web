WeFacts

Wefacts (weather-facts) makes it easy for researchers and developers to obtain historical weather data including temperature, wind speed, wind direction, etc.

Historical weather is globally covered, with data source from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. See details here.

Weather Station Search

Step 1: Type in the address or zip code, e.g. "5000 Forbes Ave Pittsburgh 15213" or "15213", or simply "Pittsburgh".

* Separate multiple addresses by ";", or click button "Select local file" then "Upload".

Step 2: Set the start_date and end_date for your weather data request in format "YYYY-MM-DD".

Step 3: Press button "Search", then here we go!
