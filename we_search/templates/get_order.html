WeFacts

Wefacts (weather-facts) makes it easy for researchers and developers to obtain historical weather data including temperature, wind speed, wind direction, etc.

Weather source is from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. Details here.

Weather Station Search

Step 1: Type in an address, e.g. "5000 Forbes Ave Pittsburgh 15213" or simply "Pittsburgh"

Step 2: Set the start_date and end_date for your weather data request in format "YYYY-MM-DD".

Step 3: Press button Search, then you are there!
